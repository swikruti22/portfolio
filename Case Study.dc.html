AI Support for US Health Insurance

Designing a smart AI conversational agent that reduces friction in activity reporting while encouraging healthier behaviour among senior members.

Role
Senior Conversation Designer
Duration
1 month
Background

Health insurance providers increasingly offer wellness-based rewards to encourage members to stay active. Yet reporting these activities often becomes a point of friction — especially for senior users who may find digital systems confusing. At the same time, businesses aim to reduce support costs by shifting these interactions to AI. But in sensitive domains like healthcare, overly rigid or transactional bots can break trust, leading to more escalations rather than fewer. This project focuses on a conversational AI experience that lets members self-report activity in a simple, trustworthy way while encouraging healthy behaviour.

◎

Goal

Design a low-friction, trust-based reporting experience that enables members to easily self-report activity completion, encourages continued participation in healthy behaviours, and reduces unnecessary human intervention.
